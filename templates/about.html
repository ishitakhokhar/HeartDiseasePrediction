How It Works

Three-step process from input to prediction

1

Input Health Data

Users enter their health parameters including age, blood pressure, cholesterol, glucose levels, and lifestyle factors through an intuitive web form.

2

AI Processing

The trained machine learning model analyzes the input data, compares it with patterns learned from thousands of medical cases, and calculates risk probabilities.

3

Risk Assessment

The system generates a comprehensive risk assessment report with visualizations, feature importance analysis, and preventive recommendations.
